About Me

I started coding at 13 years old and hosted/maintained a RuneScape private server with 300-500 players online daily. At an early age, I was very interested in computers and technology. At 17 years old, I built my first computer. At 18 years old, I built my first mechanical keyboard. Today, my primary hobbies are mechanical keyboards (favorite switches are Topre and Zealios), custom loop watercooling for computers, and weightlifting.

I'm currently a senior year student at the University of Washington - Tacoma and seeking full-time Software Engineering positions after my graduation from UWT in the Seattle area. Feel free to connect with me on LinkedIn, view my resume, or check out my GitHub profile.
